Last updated: Jauary 10, 2025

D Integralas ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, BreezePDF (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the terms described here.

1. Personal Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect data for diagnostic and analytical purposes to help improve the App.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services in the App:

a) Firebase Crashlytics

Firebase Crashlytics helps us monitor and fix crashes or issues within the App. While Crashlytics does not collect any personal data directly, it may gather anonymized information such as:

Device state and information (e.g., model, operating system);

App version being used;

Details of the crash or error;

This information is used strictly for diagnosing and resolving technical problems.

b) Google Analytics for Firebase

Google Analytics for Firebase helps us understand how users interact with the App, which features are popular, and how we can improve the user experience. This service may collect anonymized data such as:

Frequency of use;

Device type and operating system;

General usage patterns (e.g., which screens are viewed);

Google Analytics does not collect personally identifiable information according to Google's data collection policies.

3. How We Use Your Data

The anonymized data collected through these services is used for:

Improving App functionality and performance;

Identifying and fixing bugs or crashes;

Understanding how users engage with the App;

We do not share this information with any third-party entities except for those services mentioned above.

4. Your Control Over Data

While we do not collect personal information, you can control your data usage by disabling data collection features in the App settings or through your device's privacy settings. You can also opt out of Google Analytics by following instructions provided by Google.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information collected through our third-party services. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, and we will update the "Last updated" date above. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
